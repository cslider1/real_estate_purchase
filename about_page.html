About This Program

The City of Gotham has successfully tokenized all real estate properties within our municipal jurisdiction. This means that, in addition to traditional real estate transfers, property buyers and sellers can transact their real estate transfers with a smart contract that will be safely and permanently stored on the Ethereum blockchain. All terms of the transfer are set forth by the Buyer and Seller at the beginning of the process. There are accommodations for having third-party appraisals and inspections completed before the final property transfer.
